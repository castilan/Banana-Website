The Best Banana Bread Recipe

The recipe for the best banana bread includes 1 cup sugar, 1 cup of butter, 2 eggs, 3 ripe bananas, 1.5 cup of AP flour, 1 tsp baking sode, .5 tsp salt, and 1 tsp of vanilla extract. Mix sugar and butter then the eggs and banana seperately. Combine the two mixtures into one bowl then mix together with the remaining ingredients. Bake at 350F degrees, 20 minutes for muffins and 60 minutes for a loaf pan. Click here for recipe link!
